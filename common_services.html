Computers

Use library desktop computers to work on homework, research, printing, and online coursework. Log in with your Wake Tech key account during regular library hours.

Laptops

Need a laptop for class or studying? Borrow a library laptop for up to three hours. Students must complete the device loaner agreement once per semester before checking out a laptop.

Complete the Device Loaner Agreement →

Laptop Kiosk

Need a laptop for class or studying? Check out a laptop from the self-service kiosk using your Wake Tech ID for up to three hours. Laptops must stay on campus.

Printing

Print, scan, or copy documents in the library. Students may print up to 25 pages per day, with color printing available. Ask library staff for help getting started.
